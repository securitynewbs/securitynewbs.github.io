Our Values

Community

Our community is inclusive, vibrant, engaged, and open. Anyone with interest in cyber security is welcome, whether brand new to the space or a seasoned professional.

Expertise

We want to provide services for the community that will enable them to get accurate, fast information regarding cyber security as well as their career path.

Equity

Our community has a decided strategy to enhance equity in the domain of cyber security, with a focus on diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) and bringing in both experts and newcomers from underrepresented groups.

Engagement

We offer our community materials, talks, and panels with experts for free and work with the community to become better.

Our Experts

Security Pros

Our global network of security pros has deep expertise in most areas of cyber security, including network and infrastructure security (cloud and on-prem), OS security (Windows, Linux), cloud security (Identify Access Management/IAM, etc.), Continuous Integration (CI)/Continuous Deployment (CD) security, product/application security, Governance Risk and Compliance (GRC), Cyber Security Incidence Response Team (CSIRT) / Product Security Incidence Response Team (PSIRT), red teaming, security automation, insider threat, and Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) (various topics).

Recruiters

Our network of recruiters are there to connect the community to relevant jobs (focused on entry-level positions) and answer related questions.
